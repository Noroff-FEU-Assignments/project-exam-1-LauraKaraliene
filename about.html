WHO AM I?

I am Laura, mountain lover and nature explorer. Accompanied by my boyfriend Patrick and our fluffy friend Bachi, we wander in the wilderness of mostly Norway, collecting unforgettable experience. Here I want to share my best moments with you and inspire other explorers to follow my steps.

DISCOVER NATURE'S BEAUTY TOGETHER !

Every post I share has a uniq description with guidance and tips for those who want to visit that place. I’m not trying to invent a bike here. Same as many other hikers, I get inspiration from other travel blogs, hiking and weather forecast apps. That’s how I prepare and know what to expect. So I truly recommend to do your own research and enjoy every minute without any unexpectedness.

TIPS & TRICKS

Invest in good waterproof mountain shoes and test them before longer foot-tour. Bleeding heels or swollen toes have never been a good company for a traveler. Choose your backpack size wisely. You may need enough space for clothes and food even if it’s just a few hours walk. Weather conditions can drastically change, so dress up in layers. Sunscreen and cap - my most reliable companions in summer!
