WHO AM I?

I am Laura, mountain lover and nature explorer. Accompanied by my boyfriend Patrick and our fluffy friend Bachi, we wander in the wilderness of mostly Norway, collecting unforgettable experiences. Here I want to share my best moments with you and inspire other explorers to follow my steps.

DISCOVER NATURE'S BEAUTY TOGETHER !

Every post I share has a unique description with guidance and tips for those who want to visit that place. I’m not trying to reinvent the wheel here. Same as many other hikers, I get inspiration from other travel blogs, hiking and weather forecast apps. That’s how I prepare and know what to expect. So I truly recommend to do your own research and enjoy every minute without any surprises.

TIPS & TRICKS

Invest in high-quality waterproof mountain shoes and break them in before embarking on a longer hike. Trust me, bleeding heels or swollen toes can quickly ruin an adventure. Choose your backpack size wisely. You may need enough space for clothes and food even if it’s just a few hours walk. Weather conditions can change rapidly, so always dress in layers to easily adapt to the shifting environment. Sunscreen and a cap aren't just for the beach – they're my go-to essentials during summer hikes!

FOOD & DRINKS

Every hike deserves some culinary delights to keep you energized and motivated. it's crucial to pack not just calorie-dense, but also nutritious meals. Lightweight and non-perishable snacks such as trail mix, energy bars, and dried fruits provide quick energy boosts.Staying hydrated is key. Always carry enough water and remember that in many parts of Norway, you can refill from pristine mountain streams — nature’s own crisp, cold drink!
